A morning loop that moves from the quiet industriousness of Sathorn's coffee scene to the gold-and-incense world of the old city, and finishes with one of the most famous plates of noodles in Southeast Asia.

The walk between Wat Pho and Thip Samai is about 20 minutes through the old Rattanakosin district — past flower markets, school children in uniforms, and the occasional spirit-house shrine draped in jasmine garlands. Keep to the side streets rather than Chakrabongse Road and the city moves at a different speed.

Total distance on foot: roughly 4 km. Comfortable in soft shoes; the temple requires foot coverings be removed.
